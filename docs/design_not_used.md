## Controls

### Movement (Arrow Keys)
- ← → Walk left/right
- ↑ Jump
- ↓ Crouch
- Hold opposite direction = Block

### Attacks
| Key | Attack | Energy Required | Energy Consumed |
|-----|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| a | Normal Punch | None | None |
| z | Normal Kick | None | None |
| s | Powerful Punch | 50%+ | None |
| x | Powerful Kick | 50%+ | None |
| d | Mega Punch | 70%+ | 30% |
| c | Mega Kick | 70%+ | 30% |
| Spacebar | Special Attack | 100% | 100% |

## Energy System

- Energy bar displayed at top of screen (0-100%)
- **Time-based filling**: Approximately 15 seconds to full
- **Hit bonus**: +5% per normal hit landed, +10% per powerful hit
- Powerful attacks (3/4) require 50% threshold but don't consume
- Mega attacks (5/6) require 70% threshold and consume 30%
- Special attack requires full bar and drains it completely
